Harmonize

Are you feeling stressed and unbalanced? Do you need a weekend to rebalance and purify? This program is designed to help you disconnect from the busy modern life so that you can turn inward and learn a new daily practice that will HARMONIZE your system at the deepest levels. Give yourself the gift of a peaceful retreat from the world to cleanse your body, mind and energy and return to your community feeling aligned and purified.

Benefits

  • Learn Yogasanas and Bhutta Shuddhi–powerful yogic processes from Sadhguru to harmonize the system, cleanse the five elements and assure yourself of balance, equanimity, and harmony
  • Enhance body, mind and energy, relieve chronic conditions, and decelerate aging
  • Spend time in nature and establish a deeper relationship with the five elements so that you feel grounded, peaceful, joyful, and exuberant
  • Healthy fresh local vegetarian food to detoxify your body and boost your energy
  • Luxurious accommodations to relax and refresh
  • 5 days free of electronics to turn inward
  • 1 day of silence
