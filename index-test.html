Wellness Retreat Center

Harmonize body, mind, and energy for optimal flourishing.

Reconnect to the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space) at an awe-inspiring mountain ranch while eating consecrated local fresh fruits and veggies and learning classical hatha yoga, powerful meditation, and self-inquiry.

Contact us to join the waitlist for an upcoming retreat.

Retreats

Our retreats include luxury accomodations, rejuvenating local fresh fruits and vegetables, guided nature activities including hiking, swimming, kayaking, off-road-ing, guided yoga and meditation, and teachings from realized beings such as Sadhguru, Byron Katie, Eckhart Tolle, and Adyashanti.

Participants learn and practice classical hatha yoga (Surya Kriya and Yogasanas) and yogic methods that incorporate into daily life to connect with and purify the five elements ensuring optimal health and joy across body, mind and energy. We look forward to welcoming you to a retreat below:
