Transcend

Have you experienced the indescribable bliss of silence and stillness? What would life be like if that became your moment to moment experience, even amidst apparent chaos? Are you ready to dissolve into stillness and live a life of exuberance and ecstasy? This program is designed to help you TRANSCEND and let go of all limitations. Give yourself the gift of a 10-day deep dive into silence and the indescribable mystical dimensions beyond everyday life.

Benefits

  • Practice Surya Kriya, Yogasanas, and Bhutta Shuddhi–a powerful yogic system from Sadhguru to align with the cosmos and assure yourself of equanimity, vibrancy and ecstasy
  • Spend time in nature and establish a deep relationship with the earth, water, fire, air, and space elements so that you feel grounded, flowing, vibrant, light and free
  • Healthy fresh local vegetarian food and fasting to detoxify your body, boost your energy and help you settle into a deeper alignment
  • Luxurious accommodations to relax and refresh
  • 10 days free of electronics and in silence to turn inward deeply

Prerequisites

  • Completion of the ENERGIZE and HARMONIZE retreats or EVOLVE retreat
  • 30 days of practice of Isha Kriya, Surya Kriya, Yogasanas, and Bhutta Shuddhi
  • 30 days of light vegetarian meals prior to the TRANSCEND program dates
